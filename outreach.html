Community Outreach

Girl Scout & Cub Scout Workshops

We are proud to have helped many Girl Scout and Cub Scout troops from Northeastern Ohio learn many integral skills to engineering and computer science. From understanding binary to designing robots of their own, CWRUbotix has hosted many different groups of all ages.

Other Events & Mentoring

CWRUbotix has participated in a number of other events in Northeast Ohio such as MakerX, STEM Carnival, and more! If you would like CWRUbotix to host a workshop, assist in an event, mentor a group, or even just show off our robots, please contact us.
