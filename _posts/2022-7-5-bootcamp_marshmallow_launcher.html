CWRUbotix Bootcamp 2019: Marshmallow Launchers

This year, we had teams compete against one another in a marshmallow launching competition! Each team created their own unique way of launching marshmallows into certain bins that were assigned a point value- the farther away the more points it was worth. We had many different designs from catapults to flywheels to a crossbow!
